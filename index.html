Sergii Pastukh

In 1966 was born in Ismail city in Odessa region, Ukraine.

1991 - Master in Architecture (Kyin National University of Construction and Architecture).

1992 - architect in design institution “Zhytomyr project”.

Folowing years - architect at engeniiring companies “Strekol” and “Carling”.

1999 - designer at DIO

Designed residental buildings at “Arkada-Dom” and “SAS archbud”.

2008 - received the Architectural license, started his own design business.

2008 - “Vodoparad” object design conquest participant, Kharkiv, Ukraine.

2010 - winner in object design competition by Gessi.

Continue working as self-enterpreneur architect and interior designer.
